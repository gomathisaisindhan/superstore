# superstore
This dashboard provides an overview of Superstore sales and profit by year, category, month, and region, including profit margins, total orders, and sub-category analysis for comprehensive business performance insights.
Objective:
This dashboard provides a comprehensive summary of Superstore sales and profit data, designed for business insight and analysis.

Contents & Features:

Yearly Overview: Total sales and profit figures for 2019 and 2020.

Sales by Category: Performance of Office Supplies, Furniture, and Technology categories.

Monthly Trends: Visualization of sales by category and month.

Regional Analysis: Sales, orders, and profit margin comparisons across Central, East, South, and West regions.

Geographical Insights: Coverage includes major regions worldwide.

Sub-Category Analysis: Detailed profit breakdown for items such as Paper, Binders, Storage, Appliances, and more.

Usage Instructions:

Use the dashboard for quick assessment or in-depth analysis by filtering category, region, or time period.

Evaluate trends to support business decisions and strategy planning.

Intended Audience:
Business managers, data analysts, and stakeholders seeking to monitor and improve Superstore performance.
